# Welcome to Mountain Retreat

Your cozy hillside escape with stunning panoramic views of Queenstown and Lake Wakatipu.

We're delighted to host you at this special property! This guide contains everything you need for a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

## House Rules

Please help us maintain this beautiful property for all guests:

- **No smoking** anywhere on the property
- **No parties or events** - this is a residential area
- **Quiet hours**: 9:00 PM - 8:00 AM (respect neighbors)
- **Maximum occupancy**: 4 guests strictly enforced
- **Hot tub hours**: 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM only
- **Pets**: Not permitted

## Respect for Neighbors

This property is in a residential neighborhood. Please:
- Keep noise levels considerate at all times
- Use outdoor spaces respectfully
- Park only in designated spaces
- Follow all local regulations

## Damage & Cleaning

- Report any damages immediately
- Basic cleaning is appreciated but not required
- Additional cleaning fees may apply for excessive mess
- Security deposit covers any damages

**Violation of house rules may result in immediate termination of stay without refund.**
