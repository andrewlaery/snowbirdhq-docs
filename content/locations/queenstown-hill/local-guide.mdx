# Queenstown Hill Local Guide

Welcome to Queenstown Hill! This elevated neighborhood offers stunning panoramic views and easy access to both nature and the town center.

## Getting Around

### Transportation Options
- **Rental Car**: Highly recommended for this location
- **Bus Service**: Route 1 connects to town center (every 30 minutes)
- **Uber/Taxi**: $15-20 to Queenstown Central
- **Walking**: 25-30 minutes downhill to town center

### Parking
- Most properties have dedicated parking
- Street parking available but limited
- Town parking: Wilson Parking or council parks

## Practical Information

### Medical Services
- **Lakes District Hospital**: 325 Frankton Road (15 minutes)
- **After Hours Medical**: Frankton Road
- **Pharmacy**: Multiple locations in town center

### Banking
- **ATMs**: Available throughout town center
- **Banks**: ANZ, BNZ, ASB, Westpac all in central area

### Weather Tips
- **Summer (Dec-Feb)**: Hot days, cool evenings, high UV
- **Winter (Jun-Aug)**: Cold, possible snow, heating essential
- **Shoulder Seasons**: Variable weather, layer clothing
- **Rain**: Can be sudden, always carry a jacket

## Local Etiquette

### Respect for Nature
- Stay on marked tracks
- Pack out all rubbish
- Don't feed wildlife
- Respect private property

### Community Considerations
- Quiet neighborhoods - respect noise restrictions
- Limited parking - don't block driveways
- Tourist town - locals appreciate courtesy
- Book restaurants in advance during peak season

## Emergency Services

- **Emergency**: 111 (Police, Fire, Ambulance)
- **Police Station**: Camp Street, Queenstown
- **Fire Station**: Isle Street, Queenstown
- **Search & Rescue**: Coordinated through police
