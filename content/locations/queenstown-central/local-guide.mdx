# Queenstown Insights & Recommendations

Welcome to Queenstown! This guide provides local insights and recommendations to help you make the most of your stay in the adventure capital of New Zealand.

## Groceries & Shopping

### Premium Food Shopping
* **McKibbon's of Royalburn**: Premium gourmet grocer offering high-quality local and international products. A collaboration between the Ettema family and Royalburn Station owners Nadia Lim and Carlos Spencer.

* **Neat Meat**: Premium butchery offering high-quality, ethically sourced meats from around New Zealand. Known for exceptional service and product knowledge.

* **Harbour Fish**: Standout seafood supplier bringing the freshest catch directly from Otago waters to Queenstown. Perfect for those special dinner preparations.

* **Fergbutcher**: Premium butchery and gourmet food destination located at 7 Bush Creek Road in Arrowtown. Worth the short drive for exceptional quality.

## Practical Tips

### Transportation
* **Bus services**: Connectabus for local routes
* **Taxi and Uber**: Readily available
* **Car rental**: Multiple companies available
* **Airport transfers**: Various options available

### Weather Considerations
* **Summer (Dec-Feb)**: Warm days, cool nights, high UV
* **Winter (Jun-Aug)**: Cold temperatures, possible snow, perfect for skiing
* **Shoulder seasons**: Variable weather, layer clothing recommended

### Booking Tips
* **Peak season**: Book restaurants and activities well in advance
* **Shoulder seasons**: Better availability and pricing
* **Weather dependent**: Have backup plans for outdoor activities
